How It All Started

From a Tiny Stall
to Your Favorite Corner

It began in 2019 with a single push-cart, two recipes, and a stubborn belief that people deserved better than generic bubble tea. Our founder, Mike, would wake up before sunrise every day to cook pearls by hand — not because he had to, but because he knew the difference a little extra care makes in every sip.

Word spread quickly. The line grew longer. The push-cart became a kiosk. The kiosk became DayBreak Corner — a full café that still runs on those same two founding rules: use only real ingredients, and never rush a good cup.

"Lijinds lang nakakaalam ;)"

— Mike, Founder
Our Journey

Five Years, One Big Dream

2019
The Push-Cart Begins

Mike sets up his first push-cart outside a local school with three signature drinks and a hand-painted sign that read "DayBreak Corner." Sells out in 2 hours.

2020
First Kiosk Opens

After months of early-morning pearl-cooking and loyal regulars, DayBreak Corner earns its first permanent spot inside the local market. The menu grows to 15 drinks.

2021
Going Viral — Locally

A food blogger's video of the Hershey's Boba earns 50,000 views overnight. The queue wraps around the block for two weeks straight. Mike hires his first team of three.

2022
The Full Café Opens

DayBreak Corner moves into its first dedicated café space — warm lighting, wooden tables, and that signature smell of slow-cooked brown sugar. Coffee and food are added to the menu.

2026
Online Ordering Launches 🎉

DayBreak Corner goes digital — order ahead, customize your drink, and skip the wait. 500+ happy customers and counting. The dream just keeps growing.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Everything we do traces back to three simple beliefs that have guided DayBreak Corner from day one.

01
🌿
Honest Ingredients

We use real tea leaves, fresh fruit, and hand-cooked pearls. No artificial syrups, no shortcuts — ever. You taste the difference in every sip.

02
🤝
Community First

DayBreak Corner was built by this neighborhood and for it. We partner with local suppliers, hire locally, and give back to the community that made us.
