Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about orders, shipping, returns, and more.

Browse by Topic
Orders & Payments

Browse our products, add items to your cart, then proceed to checkout. You'll be asked to enter your shipping details and choose a payment method before confirming your order.

We accept major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), GCash, PayMaya, PayPal, and bank transfers. Cash on delivery is available in select areas.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order may already be processed. Please contact our support team immediately if you need changes.
Delivery

Standard delivery takes 10-20 minutes depending on your location. Rush delivery takes 5-10 minutes to arrive. Delivery options are available at checkout.

Once your order is shpping, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking ID. You can use this to track your order on our website.

Unfortunately, we are unable to offer nationwide shipping, as the freshness and quality of our food products cannot be guaranteed over extended transit times.
Returns & Refunds

Our return policy is effective immediately upon receipt of the goods. Customers are kindly requested to inspect their orders upon delivery, as returns or exchanges must be initiated within this period to ensure product quality and compliance.

Refunds are applicable once the goods have been received and inspected. Requests must be submitted within the designated period following delivery, and refunds will be processed in accordance with our quality assurance standards.
Still have questions?

Our support team is available Mon–Sat, 9AM–3AM. We usually reply within a few hours.
